The branches of the hospital are supervised by a relief society formed by hospital consultants and few people from the community.
The Hospital is equipped with ALS Ambulance for safe and speedy transfer of critical patients to higher centre. We also have attached blood storage unit managed by a team of trained personnel.

  • BRANCHES OF HOSPITAL AVAILABLE

  • MEDICINE: We have post graduate doctors in this field who treat all communicable and non communicable diseases and participate in government national programmes of disease eradication achieving highest targets.
  • SURGERY: A skilled surgeon posted in our community health center has been performing all basic operative procedures like Appendicitis, Hernia, Hydrocoele, Breast Surgeries, etc with good results. Complicated cases are referred to tertiary centres by him after basic management.
  • PAEDIATRIC: We have achieved hundred percent vaccination of neonates, infants and children under 5 years of age.
  • OBSTETRICS: We provide free health checkup, counselling, vaccination and diatery advice to expecting mothers. Also, we have accomplished hundred percent hospital deliveries attended by skiiled doctors and nursing staff.
  • GYNAECOLOGY: We focus on anaemia correction of females right from the school age by distributing iron tablets and medical advice. Women of reproductive age group are well aware about newer methods of family planing by trained female named ASHA by government of India.
  • GENERAL OPD: Seasonal diseases and minor trauma cases are managed by our MBBS doctors.

ANGANWADI

We have two anganwadi centres where facilities like immunisation, antenatal and postnatal care for pregnant women, basic healthcare to women and children, pre-school education to children below 5 years of age and nutrition (by Poshahar) are supplied by government to children below 5 years of age.
