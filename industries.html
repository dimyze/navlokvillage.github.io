HANDICRAFT
The village has an excellent talent for handicrafts. We work on small scale industries but produce a variety of handicraft material including bass baskets, sculptures, earthern pots, chataai, block printed decorations, embroideries, bell totas, etc The trading of these products is done both nationally and internationally.

TEXTILES
We at Navlok produce textiles mainly comprising of bandhani/bandhage work. These hand-crafted textiles are exported both raw as well as in the form of complete clothes. These beautiful dresses are created on a small scale but are worth the work.

SPICES
We export spices such as dried chilli and dried fenugreek to different parts of India as well as abroad. Women of the places are highly involved in this activity and therefore produce a big amount of these exotic spices.

FOOD ITEMS
Indian food is famoous worldwide and its rural cuisines are famous all over the world. We successfully produce and export a huge amount of such delicacies. We mainly export Papad and Ker Sangariyan. The small-scale industries in this project produce around a thousand packets per day.

ACCESSORIES
Navlok is traditionally rich. The tribal community actively participates in exporting their art in the form of jewelley items. These jewellery items are made with natural and raw material available at the locality.
About

Navlok is a self sustaining smart village that believes in quality living. It aims to be an ideal village and sets a global standard living.
